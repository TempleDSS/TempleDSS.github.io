## What Is the Scholars Studio?

The **Loretta C. Duckworth Scholars Studio (LCDSS)** is the digital scholarship center of Temple University Libraries, located inside Charles Library. Since its founding in 2014 as the Digital Scholarship Center (DSC) in Paley Library, it has grown into a multidisciplinary space where faculty, students, and researchers apply computational and creative methods to questions across the humanities, social sciences, sciences, and the arts.

The Scholars Studio provides one-on-one consultations, hands-on workshops, fellowship and residency programs, and access to specialized equipment---from 3D printers and laser cutters to VR headsets and media production tools. Its mission is to lower barriers to advanced digital methods and foster collaborative, experimental scholarship at every level of the university.

Over the past decade, the Scholars Studio has supported work spanning more than 35 academic disciplines, documented in an extensive blog archive of over 200 project write-ups, tutorials, and reflections. It has trained hundreds of graduate and undergraduate students in skills ranging from Python and GIS to photogrammetry and game design.

## Programs & Fellowships

<div class="programs-grid">
  <div class="program-item">
    <h4>Faculty Fellowship Program</h4>
    <p>Semester-long fellowships supporting faculty in developing digital research or pedagogical projects with Scholars Studio staff.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="program-item">
    <h4>Graduate Extern Program</h4>
    <p>Graduate students work alongside studio staff, gaining hands-on experience in digital methods while advancing their own research.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="program-item">
    <h4>Faculty Learning Community</h4>
    <p>A cohort-based program (now in its fourth year) where faculty integrate advanced digital tools into their teaching practice.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="program-item">
    <h4>Makerspace Residency</h4>
    <p>Artists and makers spend extended time in the makerspace creating work with digital fabrication, electronics, and emerging technology.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="program-item">
    <h4>Cultural Analytics Practicum</h4>
    <p>A certificate-level program training students in computational approaches to cultural data, from text mining to image analysis.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="program-item">
    <h4>Innovative Teaching with Makerspace Technology Grant</h4>
    <p>Funding for faculty to incorporate makerspace tools---3D printing, laser cutting, physical computing---into their courses.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="program-item">
    <h4>LCDSS Game Jam</h4>
    <p>Annual event where students design and build original games in a compressed timeframe, blending creativity with technical skill.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="program-item">
    <h4>Digital Scholars Program</h4>
    <p>An early initiative pairing graduate students with digital tools and mentorship to develop semester-long research projects.</p>
